"It doesn't look like the sick kind of blush, does it?" Hardik had a tendency to know Ishan too well, and that made Ishan uncomfortable. He glared at Hardik, but unfortunately, this only heightened everyone's suspicion.

"Are you feeling well?" she asked Ishan as she entered the room. "Yeah," he replied with a smile and a hug. They all gathered around the unconscious Shubman, sitting quietly together. The room was enveloped in an uncomfortable silence.

Although Ishan hesitated at first, he eventually relented after a lot of persuasion. He agreed to go with Hardik while a few other members would stay behind with Shubman's family.

Once Ishan left, everyone became involved in different conversations, and the previous awkward silence faded away.

Just when they thought everything was going smoothly and they were safe from any more surprises, destiny had other plans. They were sitting around when they heard muffled voices coming from Shubman's bed. Quickly, everyone turned their heads toward him; he was still unconscious but seemed to be saying something that was barely audible.

Siraj pressed the emergency button as the family gathered around Shubman, straining to decipher his words. The nurses soon ushered them out of the room while the doctors began checking Shubman's vitals. Shubman's mother looked like she might faint at any moment, while his sister appeared to be the rock that everyone wished they had in such a situation.

"No," Abhishek replied instantly, and everyone else nodded in agreement. "He just went home; I doubt he even had time to freshen up properly. We should keep him out of this for now," Shahneel added, and it made the most sense.

They didn't want him to feel tense again, especially after it had taken them hours to convince him to leave in the first place. The chatter faded as quickly as it began. Everyone was too busy anxiously staring at the door to Shubman's hospital room.
