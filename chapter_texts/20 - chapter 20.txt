"What are you doing?"

Ishan looked back to shubman standing by the door.

"Just looking at the sky" ishan says turning his head back.

"The sky is empty there's no moon tonight" shubman says coming behind him pulling him against his chest. "I was looking for you in rahul bhaiya's room when i woke up alone"

"Oh were you?" Ishan asked closing his eyes.

"You should've woken me up if you wanted to sleep in our room" shubman murmurs holding ishan for the warmth feeling the cold breeze hit his skin.

Ishan rubbed his fingers against the glass railing, he squhis eyes close, mind bothered again as mayank's words flow back into his ear from that call.

"So how was it?" Mayank speaks from the other side.

Ishan close the balcony door as silently as he can, it's still night everyone had fallen asleep an hour ago some on bed some on extra mattresses. He was laying on the mattress scrolling reels when mayank's call came.

"It was good" he let out a breath finally.

"Did he said it was a date?" Mayank asks getting a bit excited.

"Hah N-no" ishan lets out a dry chuckle.

"Did you said you wanted it to be a date?" Mayank questioned again.

"Well..." ishan sighs not knowing what to say.

"So you did not and he didn't either what are you doing ishan. Is this how you want it go? Make things clear yeah?" Mayank continues "when did it started?"

"When we were playing against Australia t20i in kerala" ishan whines.

"Ohh...i-i heard from abhishek he had a fight with sara before he flew to meet you did he tell you that?" Mayank asks with little concern in his voice.

"N-no I'm getting to know that from you" ishan whispers.

"He might have not come only for you....ishu just don't end up being a backup or a rebound"

"Ish?"

He blinked looking back to see shubman frowning at him "where are you lost? In stars?"

Ishan lightly takes shubman's hands and remove them from around his waist. He might have held onto shubman's hands longer than necessary.

Ishan can already picture the look of confusing and sadness on shubman's face.

He slowly turns around to face shubman. God should he really have the talk right now, he's second guessing himself.

"Why did you fought with sara? Is this the reason why you came to karala?" There he said it.

"W-what?" Shubman's voice is guff and shaky "what are you saying-"

ishan inhales sharply before saying "You're making it more difficult than it has to be just reply to my question simply"

Shubman's dosen't budge the silence follows.

"Alright then if you don't wanna answer that, answer this what are we shubman, what are we to you?" Ishan's voice is void of any emotions.

"We're...best friends" shubman says, voice not above a whisper.

"No" ishan shakes his head "best friends doesn't make out at any given moment, and you know how i feel for you"

"I saw the messages she's begging for you to come back, What are you gonna do now huh? Keep making out with your best friend or go back to her?" Ishan laughs humorlessly. The words feel and sound awkward coming out of his own mouth.

"You're asking me to choose?" shubman mumbled looking down.

Ishan eyes widen with a poignant sense of hurt the fire in his eyes fading out until only disappointment remains. For a brief moment he looks like he can't what he's hearing.

"Are you seriously asking me that?" His voice quivering. "Of course I'm asking you to fucking choose. In fact, I shouldn't even have to ask, are you planning to fool around with two people?"

"You don't think it's possible to love two people--" shubman paused.

Ishan freezes for a moment. The damage shubman's words did to him is unmistakable, visibly engraving itself across his face in a harrowing display of pain. "It is possible. But this- this is not for me, it's either her or me or i want nothing. So make the choice"

"Huh..so the choice is made" ishan says after a whole minute of silence. He wants to laugh and cry at the same time.

"Ish wait- shit sorry, wait a second let me-" shubman was cut off my ishan who have reached the door now.

"Let's end it right here, i don't think i can continue anymore, and as for our friendship let's take a break from each other for how long i don't know but i need that for myself" ishan says before exiting the Door.

He didn't turn back to see if shubman followed him or not.

Ishan had to let the words out, his heart was too heavy to keep going. He wiped his eyes as he entered the elevator.

Everything was swirling in his mind, ishan was glad but they should've had this talk sooner. He's too far gone but he couldn't hold it anymore not after today.

He just wants to run away but to where? Somehow he ended up at the terrace.

He can see, in front of his eyes their friendship falling apart and he was the reason, there's no way they're going back to being best friends after all that, he can't even blame shubman because he himself let everything happen. The pain of losing that special bond and the guilt of knowing the reason was him, made it unbearable. More tears come down his eyes.

.

.

Ishan feels a bright light hit his face, he tries to open his eyes but the light is too much. Slowly he's managed to open his eyes, he look up to see sun hitting right at his face. He had fallen asleep sitting on this chair, so shubman really didn't come after him. He lets out a huff making his way downstairs.

He stands in front of rohit's room taking a deep breath before knocking.

"Why are you knocking at 7AM?" A just woken up rohit opens the door. "And what's with your eyes?"

"Nothing just couldn't sleep last night" ishan says shaking it off. "I have something to ask that's why I'm here bhaiya"

"Alright come in" rohit says opening the door for him.

"I just wanted to ask" ishan looks up rohit into the eyes "will they ever let me play for any test match?"

"No. Not until someone gets injured or drop out" rohit replies looking away.

"Well Alright then this is all i wanted to know" ishan got up "I'll be heading out now"

He reached the front desk, taking a car key in desperate need to run out of this place.

.

.

.

The car came to an stop in front of a park ishan did not got out rather he sit inside trying to keep his breathe stable.

He took his phone out calling hardik's number, the call got answered on 2nd ring.

"Hey ishu-" hardik stops when he heard sniffles. "what happened? Are you crying?"

"T-they are seeing me as a substitute player only bhaiya" ishan sobbed. "I might not even get to play in any test match"

"There can be two things done, one you drop out and come back, two you stay there in hopes of getting a chance" hardik whispers gently.

"I don't want to be here anymore" ishan shook his head.

"Then tell the management you're dropping out" hardik continues "and go pack your bags I'll book your tickets for today"

Ishan cut the call and wiped his tears for the nth time today finally getting his eyes cleaned, he typed a mail to bcci hitting the send and throwing his phone on passenger seat.

Ishan was tired too tired all of a sudden. But somehow relieved. The hope died, leaving behind a corpse of him who felt numb to everything.

He lost hope the moment when he realised there was no future for him in this tese series.

He lost tow things he loves the most today two things that does not hold any future for him right now.

He knows there's no hope with shubman either. He stopped hoping for it too. He would never fall for that dream and illusion again.

............end for today...........

.

.

.

.

.
