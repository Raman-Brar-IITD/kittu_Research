The door closed with a loud bang, leaving shubman standing there alone in the balcony.

He doesn't even know for how long he stands there and watches the closed door, as if he wanted to open it and bring ishan back to him.

All he knows no matter how hard he tries he just can't seem to run after ishan.

Shubman sinks down on his feet right on top of the cold floor, and sits with his back against glass railing with his eyes still focused on the door that closed behind ishan.

Shame.

Guit.

Regret.

Pain.

Everything bad just came crashing in at shubman, crushing him whole.

'You can never have him.You don't deserve him.'  he's hearing voices in his head.

The tears come out burning him in his own regret. Shubman let's out a sniffle when he realises he drove ishan away.

Shubman knows he should have never given in to his inner longings,  he should have known his limits, should have never hoped for more, should have stopped himself from taking more than he's allowed to take. Should have never kissed ishan.

But how can he have not given in when for the past six years he was dying to have a kiss, just a kiss and when he finally had the taste he couldn't stop himself.

Six years of waiting, dreaming- six years of just staring at ishan, admiring him from afar, acting like a fool to get his attention to see him smile.

'Shubman, nice to meet you.'

Ishan's voice was deep yetbit huskymaking shubman's name sound like a mantra, a mantra that had him warped around ishan with nowhere to escape not like he wants to escape.

'Just for tonight please, you know i cannot fall asleep alone.'

'That's the last time I'm letting you sleep here.'

'Goodmorning baby'

'Goodnight darling come here let's sleep'

'Let's sneak out, the party is boring anyways'

'Where are we going?'

'Anywhere, just you and me'

The way mayank called ishan 'sweetheart' shubman tries to ignore it and carry on the conversation but then mayank's hand would grab ishan whenever he laughed, shubman burned holes into mayank that day with his eyes because all he can do is watch as mayank rubs ishan's shoulders right in front of him.

It wasn't normal to feel this jealous over your best friend shubman knows it's not platonic at all.
