Ishan woke up the next morning. He raised himself slightly to look around, only to find himself alone in bed.

With half-closed eyes, Ishan got up and went hunting for his phone, God knows where he threw it last night. After looking for it for minutes, Ishan finally locates it, buried in the pile of clothes that consists of both his and Shubman's clothes. He scrolls through the call log only to see missed calls from Mayank from last night.

Ishan decides to call back, and the call is answered instantly. "What is it?"

"This is how you greet your best friend in the whole world?" Mayank pretends to be offended.

"I just woke up. My brain can’t process phone calls right now. Stop being a crybaby," Ishan explains.

"I'm not a crybaby, geez! I'm just annoyed because it seems like you forgot about me," Mayank says with a dramatic sigh.
