Ishan tries to straighten his back as he leans back on his seat that he had been sitting on for past 15 hours and still one more hour until they land in Mumbai.

He turn off airplane mode on his phone to inform hardik that his flight will be landing in one hour.

The instant regret hit him, his phone started ringing non stop since the news of him dropping out is public now.

The notifications came like a flood.

'what happened?'

'I'm sorry to hear that'

'work on your mental health and come back stronger'

'it's going to be okay'

Reading them makes his eyes sting with unnecessary tears. Tears of realisation, they made the announcement and let him go just like that.

Still the most disappointing thing of all is not seeing a single text from shubman.

The last time ishan saw shubman was when he walked out of their room, and when he went back in to pack his stuff shubman wasn't there, he was thankful as he would not have to face shubman bow. But somewhere disappointed too he wants to see him for the last time before leaving.

Now he refuse to think about shubman.

Shubman who did nothing when he walked out, shubman who didn't even send him a text or a call.

A tear dropped on his hand, he's crying without even knowing. He wiped his eyes with the back of hands, he's done being pathetic, he forces the tears back fighting them, not wanting to cry on a airplane.

Ishan reached his apartment by 8 PM, he jumped on his bed ready to fall asleep without changing his clothes. But he quickly got woken up by door bell ringing.

"Finally, i have been ringing the bell for past 5 minutes" ishan heard hardik complain when he open the door.

"You have the pass code bhaiya, you could have open it yourself" ishan whines.

"I forgot the last digit" Hardik let out a embarrassing sigh coming inside as he ruffles ishan's hair.

"Did you had dinner?" Hardik aske going to fridge to pull out a bottle of water.

"No" ishan mumbles looking back at hardik.

"Good then i bought some take out"  hardik says taking out the plates "it's fried chicken what drink do you want with it? Beer? Whiskey? Wine? or champagne?

"You don't even eat chicken" ishan looked at him with surprise.

"Well yeah chicken is for you i got some snacks for me" hardik replies. "Now tell me what do you want to drink let's go full unhealthy today"

"Whiskey with carbonated water" ishan chuckles.

Ishan took a sip of his drink after eating 3 pieces of fried chicken because hardik didn't let him drink empty stomach.

He plans to get drunk and have a good sleep without thinking about the hangover, that's the problem for future ishan not him.

He took the very first sip and the taste of alcohol reminds him of shubman. His eyes are feeling wet again. The sudden memory of shubman kissing him when they're both drunk came.

"Your mouth is so rotten go wash it shub" ishan scoffs pushing shubman off of him. "Stop being nasty"

"Just one more baby" shubman whisper as he brushed the stray hair off of ishan's face, gently caressing the latter's cheeks, before he leaned in and placed a soft kiss on ishan's lips. And then kissing him again biting his lips this time.

"What not disgusted anymore?" Shubman asks teasing ishan, as he goes to kiss his neck now, making ishan roll his eyes.

ishan can smell the alcohol and can taste it yet it doesn't bother him at all, but he won't admit it out loud.

"I am very much disgusted" ishan says averting shubman's gaze, looking away blushing, flushing more red.

"If not your mouth, then I'll have to eat your neck up" shubman says dramatically, burying his face at the crook of ishan neck, now blushing as well.

"Ishu?"

"Huh?" Ishan blink back his tears coming back to reality when hardik called his name.

Hardik pulls the glass out of his hand putting it on table before pulling him into a hug "let it out"

Ishan let those pitiful and unwanted tears fall from his eyes resting his head on hardik's shoulder. While hardik gently pats his back.

His shoulders shakes sobs are coming out, ishan had been refusing to give himself privilege to cry properly for past 24 hours now that he got a shoulder to cry on he's letting it out.
